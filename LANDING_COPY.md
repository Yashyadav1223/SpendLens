## FAQ

### Is this a personal finance app?

No. Spendlens is built for startups and engineering teams auditing AI infrastructure and tool spend.

### Do I need to create an account?

No. The audit flow works without login. Email capture happens after the audit shows value.

### How does Spendlens calculate savings?

The backend uses deterministic rules based on official pricing, team size, seat counts, plan fit, API spend benchmarks, and duplicate-tool patterns.

### Does AI make the financial recommendations?

No. AI is only used to summarize the completed audit. The calculations are rule-based so they are repeatable and explainable.

### When should I talk to Credex?

If the audit finds more than $500/month in potential savings or meaningful API spend, Credex may help with discounted credits, vendor routing, or infrastructure spend strategy.
